Conjure
AI voice & text to 3D printing

Describe it out loud.
Conjure prints it in 3D.

Conjure turns a spoken or typed description into a real, 3D‑printable model. Generate it with AI, inspect it in an interactive 3D viewer, then slice it and send it straight to your printer — no CAD skills required.

How it works

Four steps from idea to object

You never open modeling software. Conjure handles the geometry, the preview, and the print prep for you.

1

Describe

Type it, or hold the mic and say what you want — “a hexagonal plant pot,” “a wall bracket for a 2‑inch pipe.”

2

Generate

AI builds the 3D model. Speak or type uses Meshy for creative shapes; Engineer researches specs and writes parametric CAD.

3

Inspect

Rotate, zoom, and switch between solid, edges, and wireframe views to check the model before you commit.

4

Print

Conjure slices the model for your printer and exports the file to USB — or dispatches it straight to the machine.

Two ways to build

Pick the workflow that fits

A creative object or a functional part — Conjure has a mode tuned for each.

Speak or type

Describe any object by voice or text and Meshy AI generates a printable 3D mesh. Best for figurines, décor, gifts, and quick prototypes.

  • Voice or keyboard input
  • Creative, organic shapes
  • Ready in a couple of minutes

Engineer

Describe a functional part. The AI researches real dimensions and material specs, generates precise parametric CAD, and prepares it for print.

  • Researched, real‑world dimensions
  • Parametric OpenSCAD geometry
  • Best for brackets, mounts, clips, enclosures
